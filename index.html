About Panther Neko

The panther is the symbol of the mother, the dark moon and the power of the night. This totem animal encourages us to understand the power within the shadows and to acknowledge these powers to help eliminate our fear of the dark and unknown.

The project is community owned and managed, with strict limits on individual holdings to promote decentralization and widespread adoption.

By leveraging the power of Pantha Neko and the community-driven content creation, Pantha aims to increase awareness and understanding of the biggest transfer in generational wealth in history by being the first Neko to guide and be a companion to the Inu’s such as shiba.

Key Points

COMMUNITY

From the start, Ethereum was built as a decentralized global platform powered by blockchain technology. It is has recently grown and is most commonly known for its native cryptocurrency, Ether (ETH), which the world renowned Shiba Inu was built on. Pantha will follow the same path, its initiatives will be community-led and will result in additional value being brought into Pantha Neko and its Pantha ecosystems. Everyone is welcome to join the community and help it grow!

DEDICATION

Dedication Like the legendary Shiba Inu, Pantha Neko is here to be a guide and companion for every single community member as they explore the Neko universe. Pantha is a loyal mascot for the successful Japanese Inu's , as we carve the path for the first Neko's, this will ignite and introduce new and existing Shiba Inu participants to the blockchains innovations with a warm smile and a helping hand of the new Neko era.

FUN

Blockchain and cryptocurrency technology can be daunting and scary, but it doesn’t have to be! Pantha will show everyone that crypto can be a space for creativity, collaboration, and connection. Whether you're an expert shiller or a master of the GIF creators, Pantha is a place where people’s talents and skills can shine, and where everyone has something to enjoy!
